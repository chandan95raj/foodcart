Licenses & Legal

Running a food cart in Nagar Panchayat area — important documents to keep on hand:

FSSAI Registration

Register as Petty Food Business Operator (Food Cart). You may use your residential address as the registered address. Keep certificate displayed on the cart.

Nagar Panchayat (Hawker) Permission

Apply for a local hawker/rehri license mentioning the cart's location (ward/market). This prevents encroachment notices and fines.

Quick checklist:
  • FSSAI registration (Form A) — keep printed copy on cart
  • Nagar Panchayat hawker license — location-specific
  • Photo ID, gas NOC (if using LPG), basic fire safety

Costing & Profit Tips

Rough per piece and rice costing (for your reference):

Marketing & Ops Tips

  • Display FSSAI number and Nagar Panchayat license on cart for trust.
  • Offer a stamp card: "Buy 5 plates, get 1 half free" to encourage repeat customers.
  • Use QR for UPI payments (sticker on front) and keep small change ready.
  • Keep samples for passersby in a small display; smell & sight sells.
